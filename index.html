About Me

Hi! My name is Cansin. I live in Berlin. I am really happy to be able to create this page and tell you something about myself as a fullstack web developer.

I have different background and different working experiences as you can see in my Resume. I studied Political Science and Public Administration in my bachelor degree and I focused on Political Economy of the European Integration. I chose IT Security and Privacy course in the second semester of my masters and whole new world that I knew before but never concentrated on showed up in front of me. After I had finished my masters, I applied to Digital Career Institute gGmbH(DCI) to begin my new journey. Shortly after my Web Development course started, I became not only a student but also part-time Tutor of DCI. I really enjoyed doing this job!

I can define my self well-organized, neat, motivated and always trying to look at the bright side of things. I love to spend my time with myself and my family, travel, read, meditate and trekking.
